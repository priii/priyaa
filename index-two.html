Priya

I've been in designing for two years now and I'm trying my best to evolve with it. I started out my life as an artist I do pencil sketches, paintings and wanted to step into the design world so I started improving my skill set ranging from illustrations, to HTML + CSS + javascript, all the way to UI/UX designing. Before I choose a project, I like to have a grasp on all the aspects of the design process so that I can help build client's vision from start to finish with a personal touch.

I have worked with startups, online shopping business, co-op schools and created logos for the brands, groups. I now do the job I love the most and have started freelancing. I would like you to take a look at my projects and if you are interested in working with me on a project or have an idea you would just like to discuss please don't hesitate to drop me a message.
