Groupie


In collobration with Iryna keis, Nicholas Molina. A Coursera UI design capstone project.

Groupie is a study app that helps the students who take education from online platform to next level. Apart from the mentor assistance, forum , topic discussion it also helps the students to find the peer students who takes the same courses from the same location to meet in person and to study and to do the assignments together.

Begining


In the beginning, we had lot of questions whether it has to be a system or add on to an existing social network or a new app. We brainstormed more than 100 ideas and narrowed it down to 5 ideas. We also had some challenges, there are already existing app like slack , what would make our groupie app unique.



User Research


As our team was scattered around the world, we decided to interview 10 person each from where we are located. We recruited both students and working people who currently enrolled in online courses.



our team recruited participants from the work place and who taje online courses, we realized that many of the students looks for immediate assistance and they are enthusiastic to meet the peer students who take same or similar course and to work together and finish assignment/course along with them. Additionally, we also found that student discontinue the course if they don't get proper assistance or if there is no immediate help it induce lack of motivation.

Design Process



Based on the user interview, we proceeded to create storyboard and a low fidelity prototype.

As the first step we created a paper prototype and continued to evaluate our first prototype. We did both coginitive walkthrough and heuristic evaluation. We found that we needed some changes in the design.




With the evaluation in the hand we moved on to creating our second prototype which is a high fiedlity prototype using figma.





On the login page, user have to enter their email id and create a password or they can signup with google accounts or facebook. The second page allows user to give details about their school , it could be either regular university or online platform. The third page asks for extra details




Once the user created the desired grocery list they can add more or delete the list too. Also they have an option to enter the quantity of the goods. The next step would be the choice of action whether they want to do the shopping by themselves or to get delivered.




Optimized itinerary give two options, the user want to save money or reduce the stops. If it is minimum stops, the app calculate the distance between the user's home and the closest grocery shop that has the fresh produce that matches the user's list. If it is low price, the user may need to go to more than grocery shops but they will be guided with the best price compared with all the other shops in that location.




Getting home delivery, allows user to save time and money at the same time. User can choose the optimized itinerary as low price and choose get delivered option to shop witfully. This app also maintains a profile, where the user can check the recently purchased products and when to buy calendar.

Reflection


When I did the survey, I found major cultural difference. The survey questions were asked in India and california, where as in India most of the people prefer buying their grocery by themselves and also they really like doing their grocery shopping. Only few liked the idea of someone getting the work done for them. In california, it was totally opposite pretty much everyone wants to reduce the time spent on grocery shopping. However, this app would be useful for working people, students who wants to save money and who have no time to find out the best price by themselves.
